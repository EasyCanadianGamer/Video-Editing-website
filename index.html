How I Edit

Editing Styles

Every channel has its own vibe — here are the styles I specialize in.
Long-form

Classic Let's Play

Raw long-form footage (20–30 min), primarily Minecraft and Hytale. Clean cuts, natural pacing — keeps that authentic gameplay feel without over-producing it.
Highlights

Horror Game Highlights

Full playthroughs cut down to the best and funniest moments. Think a full FNAF game edited night-by-night — keeping the sharpest reactions and scariest bits.
Collab / Chaos

Fitz-Style Collab Editing

High energy, chaotic, meme-heavy editing for multiplayer and friend group content. Fast cuts, reaction moments, comedic timing — the works.

Not seeing your style here? I'm open to other approaches and can adapt to a creator's existing style — just reach out and we'll figure it out.
